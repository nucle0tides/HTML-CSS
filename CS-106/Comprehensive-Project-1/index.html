My favorite vacation is a trip to the zoo!

My favorite vacation is a trip to the zoo. I have really enjoyed going to zoos since I was a little kid. They’re a great opportunity to see animals that one would never get to see in their backyard, learn about these animals, and capture great images. They’re also easy to go to. They do not require extensive planning because all you really need to bring is tennis shoes, sunscreen, a camera, and water! The zoo I went to the most growing up was the Kansas City Zoo. However, I have been to others including:

  • St. Louis Zoo
  • Sedgwick County Zoo (Wichita, KS)
  • Denver Zoo
  • Wild Animal Safari (Springfield, MO)
  • Deanna Rose Farmstead (Overland Park, KS)
  • Omaha Zoo
  • Turpentine Creek Wildlife Refuge (Eureka Spring, AR)
  • Wild Wilderness Drive-Through Safari (Gentry, AR)

One of my favorite trips was to the Wild Animal Safari in Springfield, Missouri when I was in middle school. It was a big drive-through zoo that provided food to feed the animals with! The fact that I was able to feed animals like camels, deer and emu made it really exciting. There was one moment when there was a giant camel head inside my dad’s car! I had to push the giant camel head out of the car window so we could keep driving. Another moment I recall is a zebra galloping beside my dad's car while we were driving around. It was really cute.
Feel free to check out some pictures from my visit to the Kansas City Zoo, the Omaha Zoo, Turpentine Creek Wildlife Refuge and Wild Wilderness Drive-Through Safari.
